Our story

Built from a simple
frustration.

We saw family members struggle with physiotherapy at home — exercising incorrectly, skipping sessions, not knowing if they were getting better. So we built the solution we wished existed.

Try the product → Contact us
Our mission

Make quality physiotherapy accessible to every Indian

India has over 5 crore people who need physiotherapy every year, but quality care is concentrated in metro cities and available only to those who can afford ₹500–1500 per session, 3x a week.

We believe that technology — specifically AI and computer vision — can change this. A smartphone camera is all a patient needs to get real-time guidance that's as good as having a physio in the room.

We are starting in Pune and Mumbai, but our vision is every city, every town, every village in India — and beyond.

🎯

Accuracy first

Our AI is built on MediaPipe and trained to clinical standards. We don't ship features that aren't accurate enough to help real patients recover.

🤝

Physios first

We are not replacing physiotherapists. We are giving them superpowers. Every feature we build is designed in partnership with practising physios.

🇮🇳

India-first

Built in Pune. Priced for India. Designed for the Indian patient and the Indian physio. We will expand globally, but India is our home.
