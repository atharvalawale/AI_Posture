Features

Everything you need to run
a remote physio practice

📊

Real-time dashboard

See all your patients in one place. Rep scores, angle data, session history, and compliance rate — updated live.

💊

Exercise prescription

Assign exercises from our library of physio-validated movements. Set reps, hold time, and difficulty per patient.

🔔

Smart alerts

Get notified instantly when a patient misses a session, scores below threshold, or needs your attention.

📈

Progress reports

Auto-generated weekly PDF reports for each patient. Share with them or use for insurance documentation.

💬

In-app messaging

Chat directly with patients inside the app. Send voice notes, corrections, and encouragement.

📹

Teleconsultation

Schedule and run video consultations directly in the app. No third-party tools needed. Coming Q4 2025.

🤖

AI form analysis

Our AI watches every rep your patient does at home. Joint angles, range of motion, smoothness — all measured automatically.

Appointment booking

Patients can book in-clinic or teleconsult appointments directly through the app.

Multi-clinic support

Running multiple locations? Manage all clinics, physios, and patients under one account.
