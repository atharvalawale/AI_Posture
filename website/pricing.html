FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial? +
Yes — all plans come with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. Early access clinics also get 3 months free. You can also try our live demo right now with no signup at all.
Do patients need to pay separately? +
No — basic patient access is included in your clinic subscription. Patients only pay if they want the premium individual plan.
What devices does it work on? +
The patient app works on any Android (7.0+) or iOS (12+) smartphone. No special hardware required — just a phone camera.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel at any time. There are no long-term contracts. Your data is exportable at any time.
Is patient data secure? +
All patient data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow all Indian health data regulations.
